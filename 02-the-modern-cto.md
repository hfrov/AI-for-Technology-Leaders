# 02 - The Modern CTO

## From Technology Owner to Business Co-Architect
The modern CTO is no longer only responsible for platforms and delivery. The role now includes shaping business strategy, driving AI adoption, and building organizational capability.

## New Expectations
- Translate AI potential into measurable business value
- Balance speed, reliability, and regulatory pressure
- Build cross-functional collaboration across engineering, product, legal, and operations

## Strategic Responsibilities
### 1. Portfolio Leadership
Prioritize AI initiatives by expected impact, feasibility, and data readiness.

### 2. Capability Building
Develop internal AI literacy, from leadership to delivery teams.

### 3. Execution Discipline
Establish repeatable patterns for experimentation, deployment, and monitoring.

## Practical CTO Questions
- Which business outcomes matter most in the next 12 months?
- Where can AI improve decision quality, not just automation speed?
- What capabilities must be built internally versus sourced externally?
