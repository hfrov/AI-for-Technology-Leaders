# Modern AI for Technology Leaders

## Purpose
This book helps technology leaders understand how to lead in an AI-first era with clarity, responsibility, and speed.

## Audience
- CTOs and aspiring CTOs
- VPs of Engineering and Heads of Product
- Technical founders and transformation leaders

## Core Themes
- Strategy before tooling
- Operating model over isolated pilots
- Governance and security by design
- Measurable business impact

## Chapter Flow
1. Introduction
2. The Modern CTO
3. AI-First Engineering
4. Real-World Examples
5. Governance
6. Security

## Working Style
- Keep every chapter practical and decision-oriented.
- Use short examples and concrete leadership trade-offs.
- End sections with actionable checklists.
