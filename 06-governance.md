# 06 - Governance

## Governance as an Enabler
Effective AI governance should accelerate responsible delivery, not block innovation. The goal is to create clear guardrails, decision rights, and accountability.

## Governance Building Blocks
- Policy: Define acceptable AI usage and risk thresholds
- Process: Standardize review and approval workflows
- Ownership: Assign accountable roles for models and data assets
- Evidence: Maintain documentation for decisions and controls

## Practical Governance Model
### Risk Tiers
Classify use cases by potential impact on customers, operations, and compliance.

### Control Depth
Apply stronger controls to higher-risk use cases while keeping low-risk pathways lightweight.

### Review Cadence
Run periodic governance reviews to track incidents, exceptions, and policy updates.

## Leadership Checklist
- Do we have clear AI decision rights?
- Are controls proportional to risk?
- Can we explain decisions to internal and external stakeholders?
